Important factors in choosing your perfect wedding venue

  • Location: Proximity to hotels and travel time for your guests
  • Capacity: Make sure the capacity fits your event
  • Cost: Determine how much you can afford to avoid the heartache of falling in love with a place that's well out of your price range
  • Availability: Are you willing to change your date for the perfect location?
  • Indoor/Outdoor Space
  • In-House Catering: See if they have restrictions on catering, such as an in-house caterer or a preferred list of caterers
  • Accessibility: Consider parking and access for guests with special needs
